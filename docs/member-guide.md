# Member Guide

This guide explains how members use the Klink app.

## What You Can Do

- **Giving** — give online via Paystack
- **Attendance** — check in for services and events
- **Groups** — join and participate in small groups / ministries
- **Sermons** — browse and watch the sermon archive
- **Announcements** — view church-wide announcements
- **Prayer Wall** — submit and view prayer requests
- **Store** — browse and purchase from the church store
- **Projects** — view and support ongoing church projects

## Getting Started

1. Log in with your member account (verify via SMS or email if prompted)
2. Explore the home screen to see recent announcements and sermons
3. Use the Giving tab to make a donation
4. Submit a prayer request from the Prayer Wall whenever you need one
